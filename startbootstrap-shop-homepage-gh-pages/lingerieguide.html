The Ultimate Guide to Bridal Lingerie

While many brides focus on the wedding dress as the key piece of their bridal look, there’s one more essential aspect that often gets overlooked: lingerie. The right lingerie not only enhances your overall look but can also elevate your confidence, comfort, and sense of intimacy on your wedding day and beyond. From luxurious silk pajamas to delicate robes and supportive undergarments, your lingerie is an integral part of your ensemble, ensuring you feel as amazing as you look.

Whether it’s about adding an extra layer of elegance under your wedding gown or bringing a touch of romance to your honeymoon wardrobe, bridal lingerie plays a significant role. It allows you to feel special, confident, and even a bit sensual, no matter the occasion. However, shopping for these intimate pieces can often feel overwhelming with so many options available.

To make your lingerie shopping experience easier and more enjoyable, we’ve turned to Charlie Rose, our expert bridal lingerie sales specialist, for guidance on the best lingerie styles and trends. With her expert advice, you’ll feel empowered to choose pieces that not only complement your wedding look but also make you feel fabulous in every way.

Q&A with Charlie Rose – Bridal Lingerie Expert

Q: I’m trying on my Wedding Gown. What underwear should I bring?

Ms. Rose: When you head into a wedding gown boutique, it’s best to bring a few different options to cover all bases. For the bottom half, seamless shapewear and a nude seamless brief are great choices—they'll work well whether you're trying on a form-fitting gown or a voluminous princess-style skirt. For the top half, I recommend bringing a plunge bra and a strapless bra. Depending on the shape of the dress and how it fits, you might even find that you don’t need a bra on the Big Day!

Q: Do I wear my lingerie under the Wedding Gown?

Ms. Rose: It really depends on the style of your wedding gown and the lingerie you’re wearing! That said, most of the time, seamless underwear works best (even though they might not be the sexiest option). They provide a smooth look under the dress without visible lines, so they’re always a safe and practical choice.

Q: When do I change into my Lingerie?

Ms. Rose: If you’re not wearing your wedding lingerie under the gown, the best time to slip into it is when you and your partner arrive at your hotel room (or wherever you’re spending the night). Have your lingerie packed in your overnight bag and change in the bathroom. My pro tip? Tell your partner to wait in the bedroom because you’ve got a surprise for them—then make a dramatic, exciting reveal! It’ll be a fun and intimate moment to remember.

Q: How do I know what type of lingerie my partner will find sexy?

Ms.Rose: It can be daunting choosing sexy lingerie, especially when it’s for such a big occasion like your wedding night! My biggest tip is to find something that makes YOU feel good, because when you feel confident, you’ll look sexy to your partner. Traditionally, bridal lingerie was white, but if you prefer black (or pink, or blue, or orange), go with it! Garter belts, thongs, and push-up bras are fail-safe options, but if you want something with a little more coverage consider a slinky teddy or a sexy g-string body-suit.

Q: What Is a Boudoir Shoot?

Ms. Rose: A boudoir shoot is a personal, intimate, and empowering photography session designed to capture beauty, confidence, and femininity in a tasteful and artistic way. Brides—or anyone—can take part in a boudoir session, wearing elegant, romantic, or sultry lingerie or attire. These photos serve as a personal keepsake or a special gift for a partner, often presented before the wedding, on the honeymoon, or for an anniversary. The goal is to make you feel confident, radiant, and truly beautiful in your own skin. See the album below for some examples and inspiration from real brides boudoir shoots.

Q: How can I make my lingerie more special for the wedding night?

Ms. Rose: To elevate your lingerie for the wedding night, look for luxurious fabrics like silk, satin, or lace that feel as amazing as they look. Consider pieces with unique details like ribbons, pearls, or crystals for that extra touch of glamour. A matching robe or kimono can also complete the look and add a sense of intimacy to the moment. Above all, the best way to make your lingerie special is to choose something that speaks to your personal style and makes you feel beautiful and confident.

Q: What is the best type of bra for my wedding day?

Ms. Rose: For most brides, choosing a bra for their wedding day can feel like a tricky decision, but it's all about support, comfort, and how it complements your gown. On your wedding day, you may feel like opting for something more delicate and intricate than your everyday bra. The right bridal bra will enhance your figure, providing you with the support you need while also making you feel confident and beautiful.

Your choice of bra will largely depend on the style of wedding dress:

  • Structured Bodices & Corsets: Many modern wedding gowns feature inbuilt bras or cup support, meaning you may not need to wear a seperate bra at all. These styles are so similar to corsets that they offer the support of a bra and eliminates the need for one.
  • Fitted or High-Neck Gowns: If your dress has a high neckline or long sleeves, you might need a padded bra for extra coverage and support. This is especially useful if you need help filling out the form of the dress.
  • Backless Dresses: For backless dresses, stick-on cups or an adhesive bra are the best options. While these bras are typically minimal and provide little support, they are great for covering the nipple and maintaining a smooth line.
  • Plunge & Convertible Bras: If your dress has a deep plunge or a low back, a plunge bra or convertible bra that allows you to adjust the straps can help make sure no straps are visible. These styles ensure that the bra remains hidden while offering comfortable support.

Q: What is the best type of panties and stockings for my wedding day?

Ms.Rose: Choosing the right panties and stockings is just as important as picking out your wedding bra. The right lingerie will provde comfort and confidence all day, while also ensuring your dress fits and moves beautifully.

  • Panties: Brides should choose seamless panties or a g-string/thong to avoid visible panty line (VPL) under the dress. These options ensure smooth, flawless lines while providing the necessary comfort.
  • Stockings & Hosiery: The choice of stockings is often a matter of personal preference. Brides can choose traditional pantyhose for full coverage or knee-high pantyhose paired with a garter belt and garters for a sexy and comfortable alternative. The latter combination is also a popular choice for wedding night lingerie, as it blends luxury with seduction.
  • Wedding Night Lingerie: For a romantic and intimate touch, a lace thong, paired with a matching bra or corset, along with thigh-high stockings and a garter belt, creates the perfect ensemble for your wedding night. This combination exudes elegance and sexiness, making it an ideal choice for your honeymoon or wedding night celebration.
